ꯁꯩꯔꯦꯡ (Poetry)

ꯁꯥꯉꯥꯏ ꯏ

ꯁꯥꯉꯥꯏ! ꯁꯥꯉꯥꯏ! ꯁꯥꯉꯥꯏ ꯑꯣ! ꯁꯥꯉꯥꯏ ꯀꯣꯕꯤ ꯆꯤꯔꯣꯖꯕꯤ!
ꯁꯥꯉꯥꯏ ꯀꯣꯕꯤ ꯐꯖꯕꯤ!
ꯃꯤꯟ ꯀꯣꯢꯍꯤ ꯍꯤꯟꯔꯦ;
ꯅꯥꯗꯤ ꯁꯤꯗ ꯂꯣꯇꯂꯝꯃꯤ;
ꯅꯥꯗꯤ ꯁꯤꯗ ꯂꯦꯔꯝꯃꯤ।
ꯐꯨꯝꯗꯤ ꯃꯔꯈ ꯑꯁꯤꯗ
ꯅꯥꯒꯤ ꯅꯌꯨꯝ ꯑꯣꯢꯗꯨꯟ
ꯂꯦꯕꯁꯤꯕꯨ ꯅꯨꯖꯖꯥꯏꯕꯣꯢ!
ꯂꯦꯌꯨ ꯂꯦꯌꯨ ꯁꯖꯥꯏ ꯑꯣ
ꯅꯨꯖꯖꯥꯏꯅꯕꯨ ꯂꯦꯔꯝꯃꯨ
ꯅꯣꯔ ꯍꯅꯣ ꯄꯨꯔꯈꯦ।

ꯄꯨꯟ

ꯍ ꯄꯨꯟ ꯀꯥꯅꯥꯟꯕꯤ! ꯂꯦꯕꯥꯛ ꯄꯨꯟꯗꯤ ꯀꯥꯅꯥꯟꯕꯤ!
ꯏꯁꯤꯖ ꯌꯥꯝ ꯁꯣꯔꯥꯖꯦ,
ꯅꯥꯒꯤ ꯏꯁꯤꯖ ꯑꯥꯏꯁꯕ
ꯁꯖꯤꯛꯇꯕꯨ ꯍꯀꯖꯦ!
ꯄꯨꯟꯗꯒꯤ ꯍꯝꯕ ꯏꯁꯤꯖꯗꯤ
ꯌꯥꯝ ꯏꯈꯤ ꯍꯥꯎꯏꯗꯣ!
ꯏꯁꯤꯖ ꯌꯥꯝ ꯁꯣꯔꯥꯖꯦ,
ꯄꯨꯟꯒꯤ ꯏꯁꯤꯖ ꯑꯥꯏꯁꯕ
ꯁꯖꯤꯛꯇꯕꯨ ꯍꯀꯖꯦ।

About This Project

This digital archive aims to preserve and promote Manipuri literature written in Meetei Mayek script. Our collection includes Wareng (prose) and Seireng (poetry) from various Manipuri textbooks used in schools across Manipur.

We are committed to:

  • Preserving traditional Manipuri literature
  • Making educational materials more accessible
  • Promoting the use of Meetei Mayek script
  • Supporting language learning for younger generations

This project is developed by scholars and technology enthusiasts passionate about Manipuri culture and language preservation.
